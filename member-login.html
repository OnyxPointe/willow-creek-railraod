Become a member

WCRR offers memberships to fit your need.  We offer Regular, Associate (Visiting, Non-Resident), and Junior (Up to 18 years old) memberships.  All memberships are good for one year, from January 1 thru December 31, annually.

The Regular membership entitles one vote in WCRR elections.  The junior and associate membership is a non-voting membership.
