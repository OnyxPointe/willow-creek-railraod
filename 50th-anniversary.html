50 Years of Railroading: 1975-2025

Willow Creek Railroad Museum was started by just two individuals in 1975. Now, out membership includes almost 100 people. We give 10,000 train rides to the public annually, as one of fourteen museums and attractions at Powerland Heritage Park. We hope you will join us in celebrating 50 years of railroading history this summer!
