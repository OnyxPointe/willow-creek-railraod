50 Years of Railroading: 1975-2025

Willow Creek Railroad Museum was started by just two individuals in 1975. Now, our membership includes almost 100 people. We give 15,000 train rides to the public annually, as one of fourteen museums and attractions at Powerland Heritage Park. We hope you will join us in celebrating 50 years of railroading history this summer!

The Details:

  • Bring and enjoy your own picnic lunch.
  • Spend the day riding trains with friends and family.
  • Private tours will be available around Willow Creek.
  • Trolley rides will be available at the Trolley Museum.
  • *Willow Creek 50th Anniversary event does not include admission to the other museums around Powerland.
  • Follow along on Facebook for updates!
